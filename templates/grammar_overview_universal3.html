Use of Nominative

This case is marks the subject in a sentence and is not accompanied by a preposition.

Muž pracuje ve škole.
(The man works in a school)

Use of Genitive

Without a preposition, this case usually marks possession.

To je auto pana Nováka.
(That is Mr. Novák's car)


Nouns in genitive case are usually preceded by the following prepositions:

- bez - without (bez doktora - without a doctor)
- blízko - near (blízko školy - near the school)
- do - to (ju do školy - I go to school)
- od - from/by (od kamaráda - from a friend)
- z / ze - from (z Česka - from the Czech republic)

Use of Dative

Without a preposition, this case usually marks an indirect object.

Dávám to učiteli.
(I give it to the teacher)


Nouns in dative case are usually preceded by the following prepositions:

- k / ke - to/towards (příjdu k učiteli - I come to the teacher)
- kvůli - because of (kvůli učiteli - beacuse of the teacher)

Use of Accusative

Without a preposition, this case usually marks a direct object.

Vidím učitele.
(I see the teacher)


Nouns in dative case are usually preceded by the following prepositions:

- na - on (direction) (dám to na postel - I put it on the bed)

Use of Vocative

This case is used to address people, objects etc.

Dane, jak se máš.
(Dan, how are you?)

Use of Locative

Nouns in dative case are usually preceded by the following prepositions:

- na - on(location) (je to na posteli - it is on the bed)
- v/ve - in (ve škole - in the school)
- o - about (mluvíme o škole - we speak about the school)

Use of Instrumental

Without a preposition, this case is used to express a means or an instrument.

Pojedeme autem.
(We go by car)


Nouns in dative case are usually preceded by the following prepositions:

- s / se - with (s učitelem - with the teacher)
- před - in front of (před učitelem - in front of the teacher)
- za - behind (za učitelem - behind the teacher)
- pod - under (pod učitelem - under the teacher)
- nad - above (nad učitelem - above the teacher)

Gramatical Gender

- Grammatical gender does not always reflect the biological gender
(e.g., "děvče" means young girl, yet the gender is neuter)


- adjectives have to agree in number, gender and case with the nouns they modify
(e.g., mladé děvče, mladý muž, mladá žena)
