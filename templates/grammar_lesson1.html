In a formal setting,to address older people or people to whom we show respect, we use second person plural (Vy) with a capital "V" In an informal setting, when talking to a friend we use second person singular (ty)

Vy jste učitel. (You are a teacher - formal)

Ty jsi učitel. (You are a teacher - informal)

Questions - Otázky

We do not change the word order in questions.
We indicate that a sentence is a question by raising intonation at the end of the sentence

Vy jste ucitel. (tady bude audio)

Vy jste ucitel? (tady bude audio)
